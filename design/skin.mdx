## Concepts

<Note>
We wanted our rover to look
like Hèviosso, the voodoo god of thunder in Beninese culture.
</Note>

So we created a skin for it to wear when it wants to be Hèviosso.
This skin takes all the main attributes associate to this divinity in
myths and legends.
